Signal Flow

How to set up the FX chain for this parameter.

  • Series (BiDo): The signal gets Downsampled first, then Bitcrushed. Good for light gritty distortion effects.
  • Series (DoBi): The signal gets Bitcrushed first, then Downsampled. Good for heavy gritty distortion effects.
  • Parallel: The signal is duplicated; one signal gets Downsampled, while the other gets Bitcrushed. After processing, the signals are summed. Good for a "softer" effect.

Keep in mind that using Parallel is slightly more taxing on performance, but likely negligible.

Default: Series (BiDo)
